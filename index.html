After blur‑simplifying the image, edges use a percentile of Sobel strength. Higher = fewer, stronger edges—usually cleaner “key figure” contours.
2000
Total points split across paths. More samples = smoother geometry and heavier FFT work.
180
Sparse Fourier terms kept per path. Raise for sharper traces; lower for faster, smoother motion.
Largest contours get more harmonics so big shapes stay accurate without wasting terms on tiny regions.
